Portfolio. Front-end developer, with a knack for color and functioning design.

My name is Jenny and I am a front-end developer. I always have had an interest in computers and websites and after coming to a crossroad I decided to take the path that was completely new. I attended a women in tech conference that allowed me to meet and hear from various women all in different stages of their tech careers. After this conference I was inspired to dive in head first into the tech world!

I have found my interest in html, css, and javascript. Being able to create a functioning web application for users to enjoy is very rewarding. I am a hard workers and am always curious to learn about new things and how to implement them. The idea that technology is constanly changing and growing drew me to the industry.
